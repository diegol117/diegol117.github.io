Diego Linares


Mathematician

I am a mathematician with a strong focus on applied mathematics, data analysis, and scientific computing. My academic background is grounded in Probability Theory, Statistical Inference, Linear Regression, and Numerical Analysis, with practical applications in data science and algorithmic modeling.

I currently work as a Python Engineer and Data Analyst, developing mathematical visualizations, data-driven tools, and analytical solutions using Python, LaTeX, and scientific libraries such as NumPy, Matplotlib, and Manim. My work bridges theoretical rigor with clear communication of complex quantitative ideas.

I hold a Licentiate Degree in Mathematics from the University of Carabobo, where I have served as a professor for both Discrete Calculus and Real Calculus. Previously, as a student, I provided academic assistance in Analytical Geometry, supporting instruction in pre-calculus, algebra, and foundational mathematics for science and engineering students.

Beyond formal roles, I have experience in academic advising, technical translation, and web development for academic and research-oriented projects. I am particularly interested in numerical methods for nonlinear equations, statistical modeling, and the effective visualization of mathematical concepts for education and research. You can find professional references and additional information about my work on my LinkedIn profile.
