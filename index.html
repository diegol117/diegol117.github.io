Professional Overview

I am a mathematician specialized in applied mathematics, data analysis, and scientific computing, with a strong foundation in probability, statistical modeling, and numerical analysis.

I currently work as a Python Engineer and Data Analyst, developing mathematical visualizations using Python, LaTeX, and scientific libraries such as NumPy, Matplotlib, and Manim.

I hold a Licentiate Degree in Mathematics from the University of Carabobo, where I have served as a professor for both Discrete Calculus and Real Calculus.

Previously, as a student, I provided academic assistance in Analytical Geometry, supporting instruction in pre-calculus, algebra, and foundational mathematics for science and engineering students.

My interests include numerical methods for nonlinear equations, statistical modeling, and the effective visualization of mathematical concepts for education and research.
